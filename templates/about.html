About SnowPack Map:

SnowPack Map was created to make adventure planning easier! SnowPack Map pulls data from the NOAA NOHRSC snow data page. By default, the map shows the latest available data from NOAA. Users can also look at historical snow data (going back to 2012)! Have fun finding snow… or avoiding it.

SnowPack Map was created by Jackie Kinsler as her Hackbright Academy capstone project.

Background photo: Mt. Shuksan by Matt Millard
